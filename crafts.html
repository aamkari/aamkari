THE CRAFTS

The Soul Behind Every AAMKARI Creation

Every AAMKARI garment begins long before it reaches the hands of its wearer. It begins with artisans, heritage, looms, threads and centuries of Indian craftsmanship carefully preserved for today.

Chanderi

Known for its feather-light elegance and subtle sheen, Chanderi has been woven in Madhya Pradesh for centuries. Its transparency and luxurious texture make it one of India's most celebrated heritage textiles.

Maheshwari

Originating from Maheshwar, this fabric beautifully balances cotton and silk, creating graceful garments that remain light, comfortable and timeless.

Bagh Printing

Hand block printed using natural dyes, Bagh printing reflects generations of artistic precision rooted in Madhya Pradesh.

Hand Embroidery

Every stitch celebrates patience, craftsmanship and human hands, making every AAMKARI piece quietly unique.

Natural Dyeing

Colours inspired by nature preserve both the beauty of the fabric and the traditions passed from one generation to another.
